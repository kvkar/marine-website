What We Do ?

Leading Sea Food Exporter in India

'River Fresh Products' Featured by SiliconIndia Magazine as Top 10 Most Promising Seafood Companies 2019 from India

M/s River Fresh Products is based in scenic and picturesque west coast of India, “Ratnagiri”. We are one of the leading supplier of quality Sea-Caught & Aquaculture material in India. We cater to domestic and international market requirement.

Our Company has a good strong backward linkage with the Fishermen, Farmers, Self-help Groups, Fishermen Co-Operative Society and different Farming Groups. In Seafood Processing industry, the input as well as Output products are perishable. So this effective links add value to our brand and thus results in superior quality of products as well as Improved Efficiency and makes operations economical.

We have vast network for sourcing all over India. We procure fish from Fishing Harbors in Gujarat, Maharashtra, Goa, Karnataka, Tamilnadu, Andhra Pradesh and Kerala, wherein we have our own collection centers equipped with all necessary infrastructure and trained Workforce for selection of Quality material and Online Inspection at the Fishing harbor or Farm Gate. We have aquaculture farming contracts in Andhra Pradesh, Maharashtra and Gujarat. We have state of the art pre-processing center which facilitates fish cleaning and cutting in Ratnagiri, Maharashtra.

We produce 200 MT of ice per day in our Two block Ice units in located in Ratnagiri MIDC Area, further we supply this to our Contract Fishing Boats in Ratnagiri so that the quality of catch is not degraded. All the necessary nutrients are safe guarded. Hence fish procured from us is healthy, nutritious, fresh and offers value for your hard earned money

We have our State of the Art Fish Processing Unit Located at Ratnagiri Industrial Development Area with all the necessary infrastructure needed for processing quality products at the least possible time. Our Factory is HACCP Certified, EU Approved & USFDA Certified. Our facility adheres to different International Quality Norms in all processes so as to maintain a high Quality Standard and at the same time preserve the wellbeing of our staff. We also have our In-house Water Treatment Plant and also Effluent Treatment Plant so as to treat the Industrial Effluents before Discharge. Our Core Services

PRE-PROCESSING

Pre-processing activities of the firm is undertaken in factory, due care is given and it is seen that all the essential steps are taken to preserve the freshness and quality of the catches.

ICE PLANT

It commenced its first operations with capacity of 45 Tons/Day. In second phase capacity of 65 Tons/Day & now with capacity at 110 Tons/Day making it one of the biggest ice plants in Ratnagiri.
